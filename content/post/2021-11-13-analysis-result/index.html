Analysis on Lending data of Australia

Background

Based on data disclosed by Australia Bureau of Statistics, in 2018, lending to household has reached a lowest point since 2015 and lending to commercial kept on fluctuating during 2015 to 2018.

Lending to household, or lending to individual provides a general estimation on citizenâ€™s willingness on consumption. With a sharp decrease on household lending, there should be a clear trend on national consumption willingness.

On the other hand, lending to commercial provides a view on the macro economics.

In this analysis, we try to understand the trend of lending volume in 2018 to generate some insignt on the macro economics.

Data Preparation

Data source of this analysis comes from Australia Bureau of Statistics. This contains the value of commitment of 59 types of Loan Purpose under 4 main Categories are contained. Below table1 shows a partial list of the loan purpose.

The main purpose of this analysis is to understand the relationship between loan commitment and some economic estimators.

As Australia government only disclosed CPI (Consumer Price Inflation) estimator every three months, it would not be easy to compare CPI with personal loan commitment in one plot.

From graph below4, we can see a significant decline during 2018, which fits the decrease of Personal Loan commitment.

Conclusion

In conclusion, personal loan commitment is a way to reflect the Consumer Price Inflaction. These two estimators are highly correlated and shows the trend of citizen consumption level.
